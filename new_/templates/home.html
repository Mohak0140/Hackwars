AI Diagnostic Feature

EarlyAid uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze medical data and provide early detection of various diseases. By processing symptoms, medical history, and other parameters, our tool helps doctors and healthcare providers make informed decisions quickly. The AI can detect early signs of diseases such as Alzheimer's, heart disease, lung disease, Parkinson's, and cancer, improving patient outcomes.

Diseases

Heart Disease
Heart disease can manifest in various forms, including coronary artery disease, arrhythmias, heart failure, and more.

Lung Disease
Lung diseases are categorized into airway diseases (e.g., asthma, COPD), lung tissue diseases (e.g., pulmonary fibrosis), circulatory disorders (e.g., pulmonary hypertension), infections (e.g., pneumonia, TB), and lung cancer. These conditions affect breathing, oxygen exchange, or blood flow, often leading to symptoms like cough and shortness of breath.

Parkinson
Parkinson's disease is a condition that affects the brain, making it harder to control movement over time. It happens when certain brain cells stop producing enough dopamine, a chemical that helps with movement and coordination.

Kidney Disease
Kidney diseases are conditions where the kidneys fail to filter waste and fluids effectively. Common types include chronic kidney disease and kidney stones. Early detection and proper care are vital for effective management.
